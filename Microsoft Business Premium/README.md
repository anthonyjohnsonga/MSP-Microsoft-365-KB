# Microsoft Business Premium

This folder contains reference materials and presentations related to Microsoft 365 Business Premium, covering its value as an MSP managed services platform for SMB clients.

---

### [Why Microsoft Business Premium (PDF)](./Why%20Microsoft%20Business%20Premium.pdf)

An 11-slide MSP management discussion deck making the case for Microsoft 365 Business Premium as the recommended standard license for managed SMB clients. The presentation covers the executive case for standardizing on Business Premium (including how it creates recurring revenue, project opportunities, and client stickiness), a comparison of Business plans (Basic, Standard, Premium, and E3/E5), the full Business Premium control plane across identity, devices, endpoint, email, data, Windows, and operations, a technical breakout of what MSP technicians can implement in each area, QBR and vCIO reporting value, a practical implementation roadmap, common management objections with responses, and a recommended management decision to adopt Business Premium as the MSP standard for managed SMB clients.
